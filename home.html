SNAPEAT

Snapeat is a smart and user-friendly food delivery application designed to connect users with their favorite nearby restaurants. The app allows customers to browse a wide range of cuisines, place customized food orders, make secure payments, and track their orders in real time.

With features like easy login, advanced search filters, order history, and reviews, Snapeat ensures a smooth and satisfying food ordering experience. Restaurants and delivery partners also get dedicated panels to manage menus, orders, and deliveries efficiently.

Snapeat aims to deliver delicious meals at your doorstep with speed, safety, and convenience.
